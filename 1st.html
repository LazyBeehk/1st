Ayush & Suchitra — 1 Year of Us

A year of stolen glances, midnight messages, shy smiles and the warmth of two hands held tight. Happy 1st anniversary, Buddhu ❤️

How it all began

It started in class 9 at DAV Public School, Simdega — the day Ayush saw Suchitra walk into the classroom with a smile that felt like sunlight. From that moment, Ayush's heart knew something special had arrived. He watched her quietly in class, until one day his friend Aman asked her about him — and discovered she liked him too.

They moved from shy glances to late-night messages. On 10th November, at 1 AM, on Ayush's birthday, a simple "Happy Birthday" message changed everything. He proposed. She said yes. That night began a year full of simple joys: walks to Bar Pani, sunset moments at Shank Nadi, and the memory of their first hug and kiss on 26 November.

"That smile became his reason. That day became the start of everything beautiful."

One-year in words

Every morning you became the first thought, every night the sweetest memory. Together we learned to laugh louder, dream bigger, and hold on tighter. Thank you for being my Buddhu — for the small talks, the silent comforts, and the love that grows stronger with each passing day.

— Forever yours, Ayush
